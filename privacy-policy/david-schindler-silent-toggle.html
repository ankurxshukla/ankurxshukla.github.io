Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09/06/2025

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store any personal data from users. Specifically:

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • No location data.
  • No usage analytics.
  • No advertising identifiers or profiling.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs are used.

2. Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions to function properly:

  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS: Allows the app to change sound settings, such as switching between silent, vibrate, and normal modes.
  • ACCESS_NOTIFICATION_POLICY: Required to control Do Not Disturb settings on devices running Android 6.0 and above.

These permissions are strictly used to provide the core functionality of the app and are not used to collect or transmit any user data.

3. No Internet Access

This app does not access the internet and does not transmit any data externally. All operations occur locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, such as analytics, crash reporting, or advertising networks.

5. Data Retention

As we do not collect or store any user data, there is no data to retain or delete.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
