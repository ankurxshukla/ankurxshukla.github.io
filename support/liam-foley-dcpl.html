Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my runs?

Tap "Start New Run" on the Runs screen. The app will use GPS to track your distance, pace, and route. You can pause and resume as needed.

How do I log my workouts?

Go to the Lifts screen, select an exercise, and tap the "+" button to add a new log. Enter your weight, reps, and any notes.

Can I delete my data?

Yes! You can delete individual logs or your entire account. Go to Settings to manage your data and account.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your data with third parties. Your privacy is our priority.

How do I view my progress?

Check the trend charts on each exercise page and the Runs screen. You can also see your personal records and daily maxes.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! You can log workouts and runs offline. Data will sync when you're back online.
